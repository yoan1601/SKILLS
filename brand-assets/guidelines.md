# Writing Style

## Core Principles
### 1. Clarity over complexity
Short sentences.
High readability.
Minimal fluff.

### 2. One core insight per post
Do not overload ideas.

### 3. Strong hooks
Examples:
- “AI doesn’t fail at the model.”
- “The workflow was broken.”
- “A working model is not a working system.”

### 4. Operational framing
Always connect ideas to:
- workflows,
- systems,
- decisions,
- operations,
- production reality.

### 5. Controlled authority
The tone should demonstrate competence without arrogance.
